About

Jan Zilinsky studies political economy and social media. He is a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Politics, Data Fellow at NYU’s College of Arts and Sciences, and Research Associate at the Social Media and Political Participation Lab.

Current research topics are:

  • What do voters remember during election campaigns?
  • What aspects of the economy are voters paying attention to?
  • What can we learn about political mobilization of cultural elites from text data?
